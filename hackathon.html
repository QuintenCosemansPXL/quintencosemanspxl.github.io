EcoTrack - TechCrunch Disrupt Hackathon

Date: September 2023

Team: 4 members

Role: Frontend Developer & Presenter

Tech Stack: React Native, Firebase, Google Maps API

Achievement: 2nd Place Overall

An app that helps users track and reduce their carbon footprint through daily activities.

Key features:

  • Real-time carbon footprint calculation
  • Personalized recommendations for reducing emissions
  • Community challenges and leaderboards
  • Integration with transportation and utility services

Our team developed this solution in just 48 hours and received recognition for its innovative approach to environmental sustainability.
